Understanding Gender

Gender is a complex and nuanced aspect of human identity that goes beyond binary classifications.

Gender Identity

One's internal, deeply held sense of gender that may or may not match the sex assigned at birth.

Gender Expression

How one presents gender through behavior, clothing, hairstyle, voice, and other external characteristics.

Gender Spectrum

The concept that gender exists beyond a simple binary of male and female, encompassing a wide range of identities.
